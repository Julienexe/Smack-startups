For Vendors

If you’re a weevil with a product or service to vend, register with us via the “create a stall” section.

Listing products on the site is free, and there’s only a 10% fee deducted off the listed price whenever you make a sale. This is for purposes of maintaining this initiative’s operations.

For shoppers & prospective buyer

To purchase any product from any store or refer prospective buyers, is as simple as browsing the categories of products, and choosing a preferred stall, or better yet opting for a trending section listed as “most frequented.

There is also an option to reach out with an inquiry/query to a stall proprietor, or to even make suggestions of prospective products in a section or stall.
